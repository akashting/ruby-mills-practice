OUR LAGACY

Back when there were no televisions, computers, helicopters, or nuclear reactors and Indian independence was still a distant dream, the looms at the Ruby Mills were already weaving the future of the fabric business. Beyond product quality and consistency, Ruby is known for its relentless pursuit of innovation. Exploration and experiments with new blends refined through state-of-the art processing and finishing technology has put us at the forefront of fabric engineering and interlining excellence.
As we look forward to another fascinating century of work, we would like to express our heartfelt gratitude to all our consumers, suppliers, employees, and partners, for a hundred years of love and trust.

OUR VALUES

Social Responsibility

A business is an integral part of the society and hence it must give back to society. Shri Manubhai Shah’s values of upliftment of the underprivileged through health care, education, senior citizen care and animal welfare remain deeply ingrained in us at Ruby and guide our CSR activities.

Vision

Vision is the rare ability to see opportunities that others can’t. At Ruby, we take pride in the fact that we can sense the advent of futuristic fibers, fabrics and invest in building the capability to handle these fibers to match the best in the textile world.

Passion For Fabrics

We don't like anything average. We want to innovate fabrics that are world-class and we apply every theory, every experience and experiment to make sure that we can achieve greatness. Passion for Fabrics is a part of our DNA.

Manufacturing expertise

Expertise is command. Expertise is accuracy. Expertise is control. Expertise is consistency. Expertise helps us do things that others deem impossible.

Innovation

Innovation requires focus, time, dedication, and an experiment-driven attitude. Exploration and innovation work on new blends, refined with state-of-the-art processing and finishing technology, has put us at the forefront of Fabric Engineering, thus making innovation a value that drives us.

Quality

Quality is world-class shrinkage control. Quality is the finish that lasts, wash after wash. Quality is perfectly distributed adhesive on interlining. Quality is the flow, the bounce in the fabric. Quality is pushing the whiteness index up there and making the fabrics stay as white for years to come. Quality is the brilliance shining through thousands Of colours. Quality is what keeps pilling at bay, while the world wonders how we do it.

Sustainability

Right from best practices in effluent treatments to enabling far-flung villages with water accessibility for agricultural development, everything we do is sustainable, ethical, and responsible. We have ensured that our product designs are also sustainable.
Our inspiration

Shri Manubhai Shah

Built on the solid foundation of technical expertise, impeccable product quality, and non-negotiable business ethics, the Ruby Mills have been the hallmark of textile innovation in India since 1917. This is the result of the leadership of our Chairman Emeritus Shri Manubhai Shah, who spent 66 years embedding these qualities in our DNA.


Shri Manharlal Chunilal Shah was an exceptional human being and an outstanding leader. A towering personality in the Indian textile industry, he was also the chairman of the Bombay Mill Owners’ Association and the Bombay Textile Research Association. Shri Manubhai was a true visionary, a man far ahead of his times; he epitomised a dauntless entrepreneurial spirit.


Apart from his business interests, he was also deeply involved in numerous philanthropic acts. His sterling leadership qualities, remarkable foresight, uncompromising pursuit of excellence, humility, and phenomenal capacity to motivate and trust others set him apart. His values and ethics continue to inspire the way Ruby does business today.


As a pioneer in textile design, we believe in sharing knowledge to improve the quality of products across the industry. Our management has even been a part of the leadership of prestigious industry bodies such as the Confederation of Indian Textile Industry.
